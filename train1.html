Eurostar e320

The Eurostar e320, part of the Siemens Velaro family, is a high-speed train designed to connect major European cities with speed, style, and efficiency. This train model, named for its top speed of 320 km/h (199 mph), was introduced to the Eurostar fleet in 2015 to improve travel times and passenger comfort on routes between London, Paris, Brussels, and beyond. The e320 is notable for its spacious and modern interiors, which accommodate up to 900 passengers—20% more than its predecessors. Equipped with state-of-the-art onboard Wi-Fi and electrical outlets at every seat, the train is built to offer an enhanced travel experience. Additionally, the e320 meets stringent safety standards and is eco-friendly, reducing energy consumption per passenger significantly. It also features a unique ability to operate on multiple railway systems, which is crucial for seamless cross-border travel within Europe.

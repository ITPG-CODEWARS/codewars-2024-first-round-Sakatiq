Eurostar e320

The Eurostar E320, also known as the Class 374, is a modern high-speed train that connects London with major European cities, including Paris, Brussels, and Amsterdam. Launched in 2015, these trains can reach speeds of up to 320 km/h (approximately 199 mph) and are designed for efficiency, operating on both high-speed and conventional tracks.

The E320 features a comfortable interior with spacious seating, large windows, and multiple service classes, including Standard, Standard Premier, and Business Premier. Passengers benefit from amenities such as complimentary Wi-Fi, power outlets, and onboard dining options.

The train's aerodynamic design reduces air resistance and noise, providing a smoother ride. The Eurostar E320 plays a vital role in enhancing international travel, offering a fast, convenient, and sustainable alternative to flying or driving between the UK and mainland Europe.

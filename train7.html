Alstom Coradia

The Alstom Coradia is a versatile family of regional and intercity trains designed by Alstom to provide efficient and sustainable rail services across Europe and beyond. Known for their adaptability and modular design, Coradia trains come in various models, including the Coradia Lint, Coradia Continental, and Coradia iLint. They operate at speeds of up to 160 km/h (99 mph), providing reliable transport across both urban and rural routes. The Coradia series is particularly valued for its flexibility, with configurations available for single- or double-decker models, different passenger capacities, and various levels of onboard comfort. A standout model in the Coradia range is the Coradia iLint, the world’s first hydrogen fuel cell passenger train, which emits only water vapor and offers a clean, quiet alternative to diesel-powered trains. Coradia trains are also equipped with modern amenities, including air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and accessibility features to enhance passenger comfort. Built with sustainability in mind, the Coradia range is energy-efficient and focuses on reducing operational costs for operators while supporting eco-friendly transit solutions for communities worldwide.
